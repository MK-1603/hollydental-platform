========================================================================
Hollyhill Dental Clinic Platform - The Complete App Workflow
========================================================================

*NOTE: This entire seamless dual-user journey was executed and built entirely by Google Antigravity.*

How does the platform actually feel to use? We built the flow to be completely intuitive for anxious patients, while remaining ridiculously fast and powerful for Dr. Roghay and the clinic staff.

Here is the exact, step-by-step journey mapped out for both roles.

1. First Impressions (Splash & Dual Login)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
When a user opens the app, they are greeted by a pristine, professional clinic logo. There is a simple, dual-tab login box: "Patient Portal" and "Staff Access". Depending on how they log in, the backend issues a secure JWT cookie and routes them instantly to their respective command center.

[ User Opens App ]
        |
        v
[ Dual-Tab Login Screen ]
   /                 \
[ Patient ]       [ Staff ]

2. PATIENT JOURNEY: The Dashboard (Home Base)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Once a patient logs in, they land on their dashboard. It summarizes everything: Upcoming Appointments, Pending Requests, and Unread Notifications. 
They have clear Quick Actions: "Request Appointment", "My Invoices", or "Contact Clinic".

      [ THE PATIENT DASHBOARD ]
      /             |             \
[ Request   ] [ View       ] [ Contact    ]
[ Booking   ] [ Invoices   ] [ Clinic     ]

3. PATIENT JOURNEY: The Interactive Dental Chart
------------------------------------------------------------------------
A patient taps "My Dental Chart". They see a massive, high-definition SVG map of their 32 adult teeth. Healthy teeth are green, fillings are blue. They tap their top-left molar, and a sleek side-panel slides in showing Dr. Roghay's exact diagnostic notes from their last visit. It is transparent and empowering.

[ Tap 'My Dental Chart' ] ---> [ View SVG Map ] ---> [ Tap Tooth ] ---> [ View Clinical Notes ]

4. ADMIN JOURNEY: The Command Center
------------------------------------------------------------------------
When the clinic staff logs in, they land on the Approvals Queue and Schedule view. They instantly see a list of pending patient booking requests. With massive green and red buttons, they hit "Approve", which instantly secures the slot on the interactive daily calendar and notifies the patient.

[ Staff Dashboard ] ---> [ View Pending Requests ] ---> [ Hit 'Approve' ] ---> [ Slot Secured in Calendar ]

5. ADMIN JOURNEY: Live Messaging & Scribe Tools
------------------------------------------------------------------------
During the day, a patient messages the clinic. The "Live Messaging Center" tab auto-refreshes, showing the new message. The staff member replies instantly. 
Later, the staff uses the AI Scribe tool. They click "Generate Clinical Brief," select the patient, and Gemini AI instantly summarizes the patient's entire medical history into a highly readable paragraph.

[ Patient Sends Msg ] ---> [ Staff Screen Auto-Refreshes ] ---> [ Instant Reply Sent ]

6. Post-Appointment & Data Export
------------------------------------------------------------------------
After a treatment, the dentist clicks "Bill Inline". An invoice is instantly generated and pushed to the Patient's portal. 
If the patient decides to leave the clinic in the future, they go to Settings and hit "Export GDPR Data". The app instantly compiles their entire history into a clean JSON file.

[ Tap 'Bill Inline' ] ---> [ Invoice Sent ] ---> [ Patient Pays via Portal ]
